katamoiran rpgs

a collection of simple solo rpgs




The rpgs collected here are designed for solo play. You can learn more about that process at my solo gaming & game design blog, katamoiran.

"Lite" rpgs are rules light, narrative solo games designed for open-ended play. "Superlite" rpgs are one page solo microRPGs.

The "Challenge" section is updated every month with a new typeset superlite RPG. My goal is to make it a whole year!



Leave a comment on my blog (slower) or on the google+ group (faster) if you want to reach me.
